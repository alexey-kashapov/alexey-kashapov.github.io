RecyBot

main page

RecyBot is a collaborative project between Skoltech and MIT. The objective is to develop a fully autonomous robotics system for smartphone dissasemling. The whole project is very big and consists of subtasks such as motion control, decision making system, perception and etc. I participated in that project and focused on perception subtask. The objective is to find the objects of interests in the input image and localize their position relative to camera frame.
