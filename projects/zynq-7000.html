Automatization of quality control of grown scintillation crystals via Zynq-7000

main page

Scintillation crystal is material which is used to create detectors of ionizing radiation.

While I was studing my bachelor degree I was working on my bachelor thesis in Budker Institute of Nuclear Physicis in Novosibirsk (BINP). In this Institute was an idea to grow scintillators for future detectors creating or selling on epxport. One of the main customer should have been Japan.

BINP developed a crossboard for a quality control of scintillators. My objective was to setup this crossboard, where the main controll device was system-on-chip Zynq-7000. It is unique chip containig FPGA and CPU on one crystal.

I developed my own LINUX image using Yocto recipes and web-server to manage the board from web-interface. I developed FPGA design for PL part of SoC.
